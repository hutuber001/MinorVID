Van A naar Berlijn

A team-project - Kwartet spel

On a schooltrip to Berlin we got the assignment to watch all kinds of interfaces in the city. I made lots of foto’s of interfaces and things dat I found interesting. After this week we sorted al the photo’s in a group of 8 people and decides which were the best. We made different categories with al the pictures and created 10 sets of each 4 cards.

I’ve learned how to work with 8 people on a project, which was harder then I thought. We had devided the tasks. Kim Garrard and I made the suitcase cover.
